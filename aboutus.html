About QuickieLearn

QuickieLearn is an interactive learning website designed to enhance web development fundamentals for students. Our mission is to make learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript interactive, accessible, and fun.

Target Audience

This platform is tailored for 3rd-year ICT students who aspire to strengthen their web development skills through comprehensive, step-by-step guidance using modern teaching methodologies.
